More about Me

I'm a breathwork facilitator for queer folks, creatives, and musicians. I support people to reconnect with parts of themselves that may have been buried or hidden away due to people-pleasing and conforming to social norms.

This work found me at a time when I was deeply disconnected from my own body, my intuition, and the parts of myself I had learned to suppress in order to be accepted. Breathwork cracked me open in a way nothing else had, and I knew I had to share it.

I believe that liberation is an embodied practice. It's not just about thinking differently -- it's about feeling differently in your body. About building a nervous system that can hold the fullness of who you are without shrinking.

We were never meant to do this work alone.

Being witnessed from a non-judgmental space, our nervous system senses this and gradually starts to return to its natural rhythm. My role is to hold that space with warmth, fierceness, and deep respect for whatever arises.

My Approach

How I Work

My approach is decolonial, embodied, warm but fierce. I create containers that honour the fullness of your experience without trying to fix or rush you through it.

This work helps with trusting your body and intuition, cultivating safety inside of you, and building a fuller relationship with your soul self, spirit, and with life.

I hold space for people who have spent too long making themselves smaller. Whether you're a musician carrying the weight of performance, a creative burned out from producing, or a queer person unlearning what the world taught you about your body -- there is room for you here.

Your agency starts right here

If something in you is saying yes, trust that. Let's breathe together.
