What I Believe

These aren't rules. They're the truths I keep returning to — the roots beneath everything I do.

Your body was never the problem

You were taught to override your instincts, suppress your feelings, and shrink yourself down. But your body has always been your wisest teacher — holding everything you need to come home to yourself.

Healing is not linear

Some days you'll feel like you've cracked it open. Other days you'll curl up. Both are part of the process. There is no timeline, no finish line — just the next breath.

You belong here

Queer, creative, neurodivergent, messy, loud, quiet — whoever you are, however you show up, there is space for you. You don't need to perform wellness to deserve healing.

Community heals

We were never meant to do this alone. Being witnessed from a non-judgmental space changes everything. When we breathe together, something shifts that can't happen in isolation.

Liberation lives in the exhale

Breathwork is a decolonial practice. It's about reclaiming your body, your voice, your agency. Every exhale is an act of letting go — of the stories, the conditioning, the weight that was never yours to carry.

Trust the body's wisdom

Your nervous system holds the answers. Not the self-help books, not the guru, not the algorithm. When you slow down enough to listen, your body will tell you exactly what it needs.

"Reclaiming and remembering who you were, before the world told you who you should be."

What I won't do

  • I won't shame you into showing up — you'll come when you're ready
  • I won't pretend there are only 3 spots left to make you panic-book
  • I won't poke at your wounds to sell you the cure
  • I won't hide behind jargon or spiritual bypassing
  • I won't push past your boundaries — ever, full stop

Hold me to this. It's a living practice, not a performance.

You don't need to be fixed.
You need to be felt.
